How to Hack @ GreyHat

This semester's Georgia Tech GreyHat Club Applied track will be a series of lectures and hands-on labs covering ethical hacking and penetration testing. The goal of this seminar is to create security-minded programmers and to encourage secure code. The full syllabus, with learning goals and other information, can be found here.
All materials, including labs, are open-source on GitHub.
